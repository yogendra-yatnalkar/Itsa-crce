ITSA's workshop on "Critical thinking and knowledge integration with data structures" held on the 5th of August went off with great success .Participants raved about the ingenious approach to thinking and how keeping a few tips in mind have changed their way of thinking and asking questions .

FIFA 2017

The 'FIFA' Event organized by ITSA saw tremendous participation and a very healthy competition. FIFA was organized during "Synergy" festival of Fr.CRCE. Synergy is a non-technical festival and "FIFA" was ITSA's main event for the festival.


House Coding 2017

The 'House Coding' Event organized by ITSA, improved and expanded participants thinking by taking part in this fun blend of "Coding and Housie". It is a game where your reflexes have to be as fast as your programming skills. House Coding was organized during "Synergy" festival of Fr.CRCE. Synergy is a non-technical festival and "House coding" was ITSA's fun with tech event.


Technical Debate 2017

ITSA and the Debate Society presented Intercollege Technical debate Competition during Crescendo. It provided an exciting platform for students to discuss technology and its predominated relevance in our daily lives. This technical debate was a fun-filled, thought-provoking experience for all who took part.
