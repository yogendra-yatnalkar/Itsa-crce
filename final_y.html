About Us

        ITSA, the sole IT council of Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering, started in the year 2011, is known for organising and conducting succesfull workshops and seminars related to Information Technology. They not only increase the knowledge of the students but also keep them updated about new technologies in the market.

         The council at its crux hopes to assist the department with its core objective of establishing Information Technology professionals in organizations that are involved in the creation, maintenance, consulting and use of Information Systems with every resource at its disposal.


ITSA is a great council which thrives to improve the overall performance of a student. The Department is proud to have such an intra-departmental council which is always ready to complete any assigned task.

- Prof. Garima Tripathi (HOD IT )
